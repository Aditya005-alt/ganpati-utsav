About Ganesh Utsav

This 10-day festival unites communities in worship of the beloved deity who removes obstacles and brings prosperity.

17th Century

Shivaji Maharaj's Revival

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and his mother Jijabai re-established the Kasba Ganpati as the Gramdevata of Pune, reviving its cultural prominence.

1892

Bhau Saheb Rangari's Initiative

Shrimant Bhausaheb Rangari initiated the first Sarvajanik (public) Ganeshotsav, uniting people to celebrate together beyond their homes.

1893

Lokmanya Tilak's Vision

Lokmanya Tilak popularized the festival on a grand scale, making it a nationalistic movement to unite Indians against colonial rule.
